===============================================
    Minimal Car Rental Terms and Conditions
===============================================

[Renter and driver qualifications]
Age: The renter must meet the minimum age requirement, often 21 or older.
License: A valid driver's license is mandatory for at least one year. An international license or notarized translation is needed if the license is not in English.
Credit Card: A major credit card in the renter's name is required for the security deposit, which is blocked on the card during the rental.

[Rental period and return]
Duration: The rental period is based on 24-hour periods, starting from the exact pick-up time.
Return: The car must be returned to the agreed-upon location on the specified date and time.
Late returns: Returning the vehicle late may result in a higher rate or a late fee. A grace period may be provided.
Extension: To extend the rental, you must contact the company for approval. An extension may not be granted, and different or higher rates may apply.

[Fees, charges, and deposits]
Security deposit: A security deposit is required at the time of pick-up and is held on the renter's credit card.
Late return fees: Charges for returning the vehicle late.
Fuel charges: The renter may be charged for refueling the vehicle if it is not returned with the same amount of fuel as when it was rented.
Other costs: The renter is responsible for costs related to traffic and parking fines, tolls, and any damages beyond what is covered by insurance.
Damage: The renter is responsible for damage to the vehicle unless it is due to ordinary wear and tear. Some agreements may have a deductible for collision damage, which the renter must pay.

[Prohibited use]
The vehicle cannot be used for illegal purposes, racing, or carrying passengers for payment.
The driver must not operate the vehicle while under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
The vehicle should not be used to push other vehicles without prior written consent.

[Other important terms]
Accident reporting: In case of an accident or collision, the renter must report it to the police and the car rental company immediately.
Maintenance: The renter must report any issues with the vehicle promptly
